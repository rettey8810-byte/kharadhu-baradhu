## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- **Colors:** Emerald green (#10b981), white, gray accents (match app branding)
- **Font:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Animations:** Smooth transitions, card reveals, number counting
- **Background:** Clean white or light gray

### Music
- **Style:** Upbeat, positive, tech-friendly
- **Tempo:** Medium-fast (120-130 BPM)
- **Mood:** Optimistic, organized, trustworthy

### Voiceover
- **Tone:** Friendly, helpful, trustworthy
- **Pacing:** Clear and measured during features, energetic during intro/outro
- **Accent:** Neutral English (or Dhivehi for local market)

### Text Overlays
- Keep text minimal and large
- Use app colors (emerald green for highlights)
- Display key stats/numbers prominently

### Platform Optimization

**YouTube (16:9)**
- Use full landscape format
- Add end screen with subscribe button

**Instagram Reels/TikTok (9:16)**
- Vertical format
- Larger text, faster cuts
- 15-30 seconds optimal

**Facebook (1:1 or 4:5)**
- Square or vertical
- Add captions (many watch without sound)
